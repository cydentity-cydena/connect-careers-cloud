# Business Context & Strategy

## Market Position

Cydena operates in UK cybersecurity recruitment, positioned against:
- **Volume platforms** (Cyberr, CyberSN) — List thousands of unverified profiles
- **Traditional agencies** — 15-25% placement fees, 3+ month cycles
- **General job boards** (LinkedIn, Indeed) — No cyber-specific verification

## Competitive Moat

### 1. Verification Infrastructure
Every candidate goes through multi-layer verification:
- Identity check (passport/driving licence)
- Right-to-Work confirmation (UK-specific)
- Certification auto-verification (Credly API, AI document analysis)
- Skills assessment (LLM-graded technical tests)
- Security clearance tracking (SC, DV, CTC)

### 2. UK Compliance Depth
Unique focus on UK regulatory frameworks:
- **CBEST** — Bank of England threat-led penetration testing
- **TIBER-UK** — Threat intelligence-based ethical red-teaming
- **SC/DV clearance** — UK government security clearance levels
- **NIS2** — Network and Information Systems Directive
- **PCI QSA** — Payment Card Industry qualified assessors

### 3. Community & Gamification
- XP system drives engagement and profile completion
- Leaderboard creates competitive motivation
- CTF challenges prove practical skills
- Free learning paths (YouTube curation) attract candidates
- Peer endorsements add social proof

## Subscription Economics

### vs Agency Model
| Metric | Traditional Agency | Cydena |
|--------|-------------------|--------|
| Cost per hire | £7,500-£15,000 | £99-£699/mo flat |
| Time to fill | 3+ months | Direct access |
| Verification | None | Multi-layer |
| Recurring cost | Per-hire fee | Predictable monthly |

### Revenue Streams
1. **Subscriptions** (primary) — Employer/recruiter monthly plans
2. **Overage unlocks** — Pay-per-unlock beyond allocation
3. **Credit packs** — One-time credit purchases
4. **Featured slots** — Training partners pay for visibility
5. **Marketplace** — Commission on talent engagements (Stripe Connect)
6. **Single unlocks** — £75/each for non-subscribers
