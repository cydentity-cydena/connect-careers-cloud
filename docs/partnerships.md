# Partnership Strategy & Outreach Templates

## Overview

Cydent's partnership strategy focuses on "fast partners" - training providers, bootcamps, and gamified platforms that already issue digital badges/certificates via Credly, Accredible, or similar systems. These partners are more agile than large certification bodies and can integrate quickly.

## Partner Value Proposition

### For Training Providers
- **Free visibility**: Showcase training graduates to employers at no cost
- **Easy integration**: Certificates on Credly/Accredible or badge URLs work automatically
- **Upsell potential**: Candidates see required certifications in job postings → drives new learners
- **Talent pipeline**: Direct connection between training completion and job opportunities

### For Candidates
- **Seamless import**: Upload existing badges/certs from partner platforms
- **Employer visibility**: Verified credentials increase profile strength and ranking
- **Points & rewards**: Earn points for verified certifications
- **Career advancement**: Direct path from training completion to job opportunities

## Questions Partners Ask

**Q: What does this cost us?**  
A: Nothing. Listing and integration are free. Optional paid co-marketing available.

**Q: Do you get access to our student data?**  
A: No. Candidates manually import their own certificates. We never access your LMS or student database.

**Q: How do you verify certificates?**  
A: We use OpenBadge standard verification, Credly/Accredible APIs, or manual review of badge URLs.

**Q: Can we co-brand job postings?**  
A: Yes, employers can tag jobs with required certifications from your training.

**Q: What if our certificates expire?**  
A: We track expiry dates and notify candidates to renew.
